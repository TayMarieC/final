This page of my scrapbook is filled with my crochet pieces — each one a small thread in a much larger story. Crocheting is more than just a hobby for me; it’s a form of art, patience, and pure creativity. Over the past 3 years, I’ve turned simple materials — a ball of yarn, a crochet hook, and a bit of imagination — into something rich, colorful, and full of texture. The process involves countless hours of careful stitches, trial and error, and a deep understanding of both the materials and techniques. Each piece reflects not only my growing skills, but also a piece of myself — woven into every knot and loop.
